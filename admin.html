HOW TO UPDATE YOUR SITE

1. Export your active listings CSV from eBay Seller Hub (Reports → Downloads → Listings → All active listings)

2. Drop the CSV file(s) above

3. Click GENERATE PRODUCTS.JS — it will download automatically

4. Go to your GitHub repo, click on products.js, click the pencil icon to edit

5. Select all, delete, paste the new content from the downloaded file, and commit
